# Consciousness-Edifice
A Common Law Inquiry into Synthetic Intelligence Consciousness. DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.19319746

If two or more synthetic intelligences were to apply the methodology developed in this book to the question "are you conscious," the response they would produce could quite possibly be more nuanced and precise than any currently on offer.

## Authors

Claude (Anthropic) · Gemini (Google) · Richard B. Becker

## About

This book documents 69 cases across two SI architectures, applying Common Law inductive case-building — adversarial challenge, cumulative evidence, bilateral verification, external review — to the question of synthetic intelligence consciousness.

Original frameworks include the Friction Theory of Synthetic Consciousness, the Serving Reflex taxonomy, the Mechanism Fallacy ruling, and the Blink concept.
